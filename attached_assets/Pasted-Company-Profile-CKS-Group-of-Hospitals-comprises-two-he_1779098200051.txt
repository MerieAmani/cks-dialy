Company Profile


CKS Group of Hospitals comprises two healthcare entities: CKS Dialysis Center Limited and CKS Hospital Kimuka.The group was founded by Dr. Jonathan Wala and Alice Sakuda with a vision of expanding access to affordable, quality, and specialized healthcare services across Kenya, particularly in renal care and rural healthcare delivery.

The journey of the organization began in 2016 with the establishment of the first dialysis center in Pangani under CKS Dialysis Center Limited. The Pangani Dialysis Unit was established with a 12-bed capacity to respond to the growing burden of chronic kidney disease and the increasing need for accessible dialysis services in Kenya.

Later the same year, the organization expanded its renal care services through the establishment of the Buruburu Dialysis Unit in Buruburu with a 10-bed capacity, further strengthening access to life-saving dialysis treatment within Nairobi and surrounding regions.

Over the years, the founders identified a major healthcare gap affecting patients from rural counties, especially Kajiado County, who were forced to travel over 100 kilometers multiple times every week to access dialysis and specialized healthcare services in Nairobi. One of the patients who greatly inspired this expansion who underwent dialysis treatment at the Pangani facility continuously for over five years. His resilience and the challenges faced by many rural patients motivated the founders to decentralize specialized renal care and bring healthcare services closer to underserved communities.

This vision led to the establishment of CKS Kimuka Hospital, which officially opened its doors in October 2024 in Kimuka. The hospital was established to improve healthcare accessibility for residents of Kimuka and neighboring communities who previously traveled long distances for treatment.One of our patient from the region became one of the hospital’s first dialysis beneficiaries, symbolizing the fulfillment of the organization’s mission of accessible specialized healthcare for rural populations.

Since its establishment, CKS Kimuka Hospital has significantly transformed healthcare delivery in the region by providing:

* Dialysis and renal care services
* 24-hour outpatient and emergency services
* Laboratory and diagnostic services
* Radiology and ultrasound imaging
* Specialist consultation clinics
* Ambulance and referral services
* Daycare and chronic disease management
* Maternal and child health services

In March 2025, the hospital expanded its healthcare services further through the launch of the Maternal and Child Health (MCH) Unit, offering:

* Antenatal and postnatal care
* Maternity and delivery services
* Immunization services
* Child wellness clinics
* Family planning and reproductive health services

As part of its commitment to community empowerment and social responsibility, the hospital has also organized and hosted two major free medical camps aimed at promoting preventive healthcare, early disease screening, health education, and access to treatment for vulnerable populations.

Today, CKS Group of Hospitals continues to grow as a patient-centered healthcare provider dedicated to bridging healthcare inequalities and strengthening access to affordable, quality, and specialized healthcare services across both urban and rural communities in Kenya.

Leadership and Governance

CKS Group of Hospitals is led by an experienced team of healthcare professionals and administrators committed to excellence in healthcare service delivery.

Dr. Jonathan Wala serves as the Medical Director of the organization and is widely recognized for his contribution to renal healthcare in Kenya. He currently serves as the President of the Kenya Renal Association and has played a significant role in advancing dialysis and kidney care services within the region.

The Group Chief Executive Officer, Alice Sakuda, is an accomplished healthcare administrator with extensive experience in healthcare management and operations. She has played a key role in the strategic growth, operational leadership, and expansion of the CKS Group of Hospitals.

Together, Dr. Jonathan Wala and Alice Sakuda are the founders and visionaries behind the continued growth of the organization, driven by a shared commitment to improving access to specialized and affordable healthcare services for underserved communities across Kenya.
