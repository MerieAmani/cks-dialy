Our Story

2018 — Foundation

Dr. Jonathan Wala and Mrs. Alice Wala founded CKS Hospital Ltd, establishing two dialysis units in Pangani (12 beds) and Buruburu (10 beds) in Nairobi.

2024 — The Vision

Driven by seeing patients from Kajiado County travel over 100km to Nairobi for dialysis — including Mr. Kutit, one of our first Kimuka patients — the founders committed to bringing care closer to home.

September 23, 2024 — Kimuka Opens

CKS Hospital Kimuka opens its doors as a 24-hour multi-service facility, immediately serving the Maasai community and surrounding villages with dialysis, outpatient care, and maternal health services.

2025 — Growing Impact

After hosting two major free medical camps (500+ patients in November 2024; World Kidney Day roadshow in March 2025), the hospital continues expanding services across Kajiado County.

Community Outreach & Immunization Drives

CKS Kimuka regularly conducts outreach across Kajiado villages — including a two-week free medical camp serving over 500 patients (November 2024) and a World Kidney Day roadshow across several villages (March 2025). We integrate health education on nutrition, hygiene, and sanitation to address climate-related health vulnerabilities in the region.
