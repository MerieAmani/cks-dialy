What We Do

Our Specialised Services

From haemodialysis to kidney transplant work-ups, every service is delivered with precision, compassion, and clinical excellence.

Haemodialysis

2 sessions per week, 4 hours each, using state-of-the-art dialysis machines. Breakfast & lunch included. Free Wi-Fi.

Kidney Transplant Work-Up

Comprehensive pre-transplant evaluation and post-transplant follow-up care coordinated with our partner hospitals.

Catheter Insertion & Removal

Safe, experienced catheter management for haemodialysis access, performed by our specialist team with strict infection-control protocols.

Kidney Biopsy

Diagnostic kidney biopsies performed under imaging guidance to accurately diagnose kidney conditions and inform treatment plans.

Outpatient Nephrology Consultation

One-on-one consultations with Dr. Jonathan Wala for CKD management, diet counselling, and medication reviews.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Dialysis is needed when your kidneys retain only 10–15% of their normal function. Symptoms include nausea, vomiting, fatigue, and swelling. Your nephrologist will determine the right moment based on blood tests (GFR) and your symptoms.

Each haemodialysis session typically lasts 4 hours and is done 3 times per week. Monthly lab tests ensure you are getting the correct dose of dialysis.

Yes. SHA covers dialysis costs for most eligible patients. We also accept most private health insurance schemes including AAR, Jubilee Health, CIC, Madison, and more.

Yes. All four CKS branches have accessible parking. If you have mobility challenges, please call ahead so we can arrange assistance.

Nairobi branches (Pangani, Buruburu, Aga Khan) are open Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM. CKS Kimuka Hospital is open 24 hours, 7 days a week — including nights, weekends, and public holidays.

Yes. Dialysis patients are generally advised to increase protein intake while limiting potassium, phosphorus, sodium, and fluid. Our team provides personalised diet counselling and monthly lab tests to monitor your nutritional goals.

Breakfast and lunch are provided at every dialysis session at no extra cost to the patient. Meals are prepared to meet the dietary guidelines for kidney patients.
